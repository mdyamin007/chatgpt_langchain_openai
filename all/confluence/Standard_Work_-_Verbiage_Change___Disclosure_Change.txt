title: Standard Work - Verbiage Change / Disclosure Change
body: ## Request

The client requests a change to verbiage or a PDF disclosure on the Admin
and/or Customer Sites.

## Deliverables / Specs

  1. Current version of the verbiage and what it will be changed to. Screenshot(s) of current verbiage preferred.
  2. OR the new version of the PDF that will replace the old one.

## Work Estimate

One Sprint

  * 1 hour of Engineering Time
  * 2 hours of QA
