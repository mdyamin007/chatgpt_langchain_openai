## Goals

  * Upgrade the client to use InfoTRAC 3.0 for MM3 and AOLP

## Background and strategic fit

Client's request. Newest standard of eStatement will also facilitate ease of
support.

## Assumptions

  * The client would only use InfoTRAC for MM3 and AOLP.
