# **Client/InfoIMAGE Fit**  
  
---  
  
#### What is Product?

|

#### Product Overview

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is a security system that verifies the
user's identity by requiring multiple credentials, providing an extra layer of
protection for accounts.

MFA with InfoTRAC provides this dual factor authentication with a one-time
pass code sent to users registered email/ mobile phone before being able to
fully log in.  

#### Features Include

  
  
  
#### Where does Product fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

MFA provides clients with an additional option to protect their accounts and
sensitive information. The extra layer of security provides clients with an
ease of mind and making them feel that their online information is more
secure.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

MFA is a valuable component for identity and access management (IAM) and an
important option for clients to be able to opt into. It helps clients trust
InfoIMAGE more knowing that their accounts and data has additional protection.
