#### What is Tax Statement?

|

#### Product Overview

InfoIMAGE produces the following Standard IRS tax forms for mailing out to
clients' customers with proper tracking and handling.

#### Tax Forms

1098

1098 E

1099 A

1099 B

1099 C

1099 DIV

1099 INT

1099 OID

1099 MISC

1099 R

1099 SA

1099 Q

5498

5498 ESA

5498 SA

1042 S

1099 PATR added  
  
#### Where does Tax Statement fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

This provides our clients with a solution for their customers who prefer/ rely
on print tax statements due to location, technology, background, etc.
