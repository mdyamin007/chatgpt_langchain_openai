# **Client/InfoIMAGE Fit**  
  
---  
  
#### What is Advanced Online Proofing?

|

#### Product Overview

Financial institutions will now be able to use the InfoTRAC job tracking
system to view individual document outputs within a cycle and provide proofing
approval. This will allow proofing online for statements, notices, or taxes.

#### Features Include

  * Search documents within a cycle by account or SSN
  * Option to have a targeted sample output file be available against a list of specific accounts for an added fee (quoted per hour)

  
  
#### Where does Advanced Online Proofing fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

This feature gives clients more agency with their document outputs and provide
better quality for their customers through proofreading.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

Advanced online proofing provides an option for our clients for additional
proofreading and quality assurance for their documents.
