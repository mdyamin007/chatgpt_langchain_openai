# **Client/InfoIMAGE Fit**  
  
---  
  
#### What is Mobile SSO?

|

#### Product Overview

Mobile SSO is a feature that provides a single set of login information for an
individual to access on their mobile phone/tablet. InfoIMAGE will read and
utilize the data in clients' back-end systems and work with their native
mobile apps to provide their customers with access to all their financial
information.

#### Features Include

  

  
  
  
#### Where does Mobile SSO fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

Clients want to focus on the growing mobile market and improving their mobile
apps and service will be valuable to their own customers.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

This will provide better quality to our clients in the long run by giving
their clients more flexibility on mobile. We can work with our clients' native
apps closer and build a stronger relationship through this service.
