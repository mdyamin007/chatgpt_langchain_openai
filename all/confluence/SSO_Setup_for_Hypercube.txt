title: SSO Setup for Hypercube
body: Hypercube as a multitenant application could be serving multiple SSO types.
Each of these SSO logins will have there independent configuration and setup
inside Hypercube application.  
SSO types supported by Hypercube(estatements 3.0) are:

  1. Digital Insight
  2. Fiserv
  3. Bottom Line
