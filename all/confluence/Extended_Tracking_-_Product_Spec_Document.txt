#### Where does Product fit?

#### Client's Fit

Clients are able to have a complete breakdown of how their mail pieces are
moving from production to end goal. This will give them reassurance and
confidence in InfoIMAGE's production quality and efficiency.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

This helps give assurance in clients on InfoIMAGE's production quality for
preparing and sending out their mail pieces.
