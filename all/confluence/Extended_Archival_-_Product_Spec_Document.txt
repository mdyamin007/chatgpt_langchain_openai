# **Client/InfoIMAGE Fit**  
  
---  
  
#### What is Product?

|

#### Product Overview

This feature allows clients to archive their eStatements/eNotices additionally
to 14 months up to 96 months (10 years).

  
  
  
#### Where does Product fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

Clients are able to provide their users with ease of access of their personal
applications over a historical amount of time.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

Archives are valuable to users for immediate access and tracking for past
applications and builds trust in InfoIMAGE's archiving.
