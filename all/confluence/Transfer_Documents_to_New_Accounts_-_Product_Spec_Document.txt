# **Client/InfoIMAGE Fit**  
  
---  
  
#### What is Product?

|

#### Product Overview

This provides admin access for the Financial Institution to transfer a
specific eStatement document archive from an existing account to a new
account. This account number reassignment will allow the member to enroll in a
new account but view his/her previous eStatement archive through the new
account.

#### Features Included

  
  
  
#### Where does Product fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

Clients will now have the option to securely move their clients' data to a new
account due to circumstances like fraud, etc.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

This gives InfoIMAGE more security and options for our clients to provide
better customer service for their members.
