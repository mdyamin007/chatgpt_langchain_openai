## Installing eGit and Gitflow Nightly

  

* * *

 **Help > Install new Software**

Copy  **<https://download.eclipse.org/egit/updates/>**  to the " **Work
With:** " field

 **Select All** and Click through **Next** to install

  

Right click anywhere in the project explorer and go to  **Import > Import...**

  

Choose  **Clone URI**

  

You should see **Git > Projects from Git**.
