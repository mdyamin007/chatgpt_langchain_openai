title: gcbk
body:   * Email subscription feature - Customers can choose to unsubscribe from receiving emails for a certain account. These are stored in the restriction table. On the PUI side, the optoutFeature flag needs to be enabled. **Note** : All statement types must be listed in the optoutFeature.statementType flag or else emails will not send out and the email job will show a "good" count of 0.
