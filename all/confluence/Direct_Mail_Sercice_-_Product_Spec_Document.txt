#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

Adding Direct Mail will help:

  * InfoIMAGE to start off with a basic foundation to rebuild relationships with clients
  * Build our clients' confidence due to absence of any variable data or complexity
  * Add more high-value work with variable data and high-profit margins (Eg. pre-approval campaigns, welcome kits)
  * Fill mid-month production downtime
  * Provide quick turnaround times
