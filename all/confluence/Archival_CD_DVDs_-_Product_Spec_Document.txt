# **Client/InfoIMAGE Fit**  
  
---  
  
#### What is Archival CD/DVDs?

|

#### Product Overview

This allows for two types of archiving for CD/DVDs by requirement, which are
searchable and commercial. Searchable allows for all customer statements,
while commercial contains a single customers' account statement within a
single production cycle. Commercial may also include searchable check images.

  
  
  
#### Where does Archival CD/DVDs fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

Clients are able to archive physical CD/DVDs over a historical amount of time
and into two categories for flexibility.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

This gives clients an option to help provide easier organization and
categorizing of their physical CD/DVDs into online archival.
