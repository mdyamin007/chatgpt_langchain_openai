**Job account table for paper jobs**  
---  
Name| Account Name (Paper)  
Account| Account Number (Paper)  
Fulladdress| Address (Paper)  
SSN| SSN (Paper)  
  
  

 **Job account table for estatement jobs**  
---  
Name| Account Name (Estmt)  
Account| Account Number (Estmt)  
SSN| SSN(Estmt)  
  
  

 **Insert Table**  
---  
Insert No| Insert No (Paper)  
Insert Name| Insert Name (Paper)  
  
  

 **Letter table**  
---  
Letter Type| Letter Type (Paper)  
  
  

 **Sent/Email table**  
---  
To (email receiver)| Email Recipient  
Account| Account Number (Estmt)
