body: # Lead

The lead is expected to be an expert in the products that the team owns. They
will transform stakeholders' requirements to scalable and sustainable design
while considering existing business logic.

# Product Owner

# Scrum Master

The Lead can be the Scrum Master. However, we can also follow a rotation
system, where each Sprint, a new person will take on the role of Scrum Master.
