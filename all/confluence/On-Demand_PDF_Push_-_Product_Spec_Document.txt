# **Client/InfoIMAGE Fit**  
  
---  
  
#### What is Product?

|

#### Product Overview

On-demand PDF push is an option on InfoTRAC to selectively email a single PDF
statement to the account holder. Enabling this feature allows the
administrative user to email account holders securely on demand.

#### Features Include

  * PDF statement will be secured using a one-time password provided by the admin (using 256 bit encryption).
  * Once the account holder receives the email, they will open the PDF attachment and be required to enter a password.
  * This service applies to all applications.

  
  
#### Where does Product fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

Clients on InfoTRAC have more flexibility and security with on-demand PDF
push, being able to send password protected PDFs to account holders freely.
Clients will give their account holders more accessibility to secure PDF
statements with this feature.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

On-demand PDF push offers additional accessibility for our clients to service
their account holders with protected PDF statements. Our clients will feel
security in InfoIMAGE in knowing their account holders are getting PDF
documents quickly and safely.
