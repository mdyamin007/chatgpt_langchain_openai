# **Client/InfoIMAGE Fit**  
  
---  
  
#### What is Mail Tracking?

|

#### Product Overview

This provides the operational capability to track full details of mail pieces
moving from InfoIMAGE production facility through various post office
checkpoints to the target destination.

#### Features Include

  * Statistical trending
  * Interactive graphical reporting(charts, maps) to monitor overall mail cycle
  * Trend slow mail
  * Track mail in route
  * Drill down to account level mail pieces
  * The ability to look up camera system images of individual mail piece by account and job cycle as proof of production/mailing from our facility

  
  
#### Where does Mail Tracking fit?

|

#### Client's Fit

Clients are able to have a complete breakdown of how their mail pieces are
moving from production to end goal. This will give them reassurance and
confidence in InfoIMAGE's production quality and efficiency.

#### InfoIMAGE's Fit

This helps give assurance in clients on InfoIMAGE's production quality for
preparing and sending out their mail pieces.
