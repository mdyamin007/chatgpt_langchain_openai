#### What is Tax Print Statements?

|

#### Product Overview

InfoIMAGE produces the following Standard IRS tax forms for mailing out to
clients' customers with proper tracking and handling.

|

#### Client's Fit

This provides our clients with a solution for their customers who prefer/ rely
