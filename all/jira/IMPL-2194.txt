title: SPCU-Transfer[32865][pvt] Transfer Documents to New Accounts
description: Set-up Transfer Stmt module for InfoTrac.



Addendum Description: Admin functionality for the Financial Institution to switch or transfer a particular eStatement document archive from an existing 
 account to a new destination account. This account number re-assignment occurs when the member/customer needs to enroll a 
 new account for eStatement operations (for reasons such as fraud, etc.), but needs the ability to view historic statements through 
 the new account.
