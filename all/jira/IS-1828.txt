 Description: Update the APY data logic listed below

If sixth digit is 5 or higher, round up. If sixth digit is 4 or less, round down.
 Round Up Example: 001106 would round up to 00111 and display on statement as 1.11%.
 Round Down Example: 001204 would round down to 00120 and display on statement as 1.20%.
