title: SUCU - [28787] [pvt] Enablen Transfer Statements Tab to New Account on InfoTrac
description: Enable Transfer Stmt tab on InfoTrac:
InfoTrac Admin functionality for the Financial Institution to switch or transfer a particular eStatement document archive from an existing account to a new destination account. This account number re-assignment occurs when the member/customer needs to enroll a
new account for eStatement operations (for reasons such as fraud, etc.), but needs the ability to view historic statements through the new account.

Dates:
ISD Start = 6/20/22
Live date = 7/11/22
